Born and raised in Seattle. Graduated from UW but still trying to figure what I wanted to do with my life. A year later I enrolled in pastry school. I have a lot of different of hobbies and it all revolves my creativity. My husband suggested I try coding. I started learning on my own and I found that it wasn't as hard as I thought it would be. Just a lot of memorization. It still is challenging but it's what I'm looking for that also incorpates my creativity.
